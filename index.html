My favourite chess players

Alexander Alekhine

Alexander Alekhine was a russian chess player and the fourth world chess champion. When Alekhine was young his playing style was very aggresive but in his mature phase he master both positional and tactical play. One of his famous games is known as Alekhine's Gun, in fact there is a rock band with this name. Alekhine was the only World Chess Champion to die undefeated.

Garry Kasparov

Garry Kasparov is considered by many one of the greatest player of all time. His style is very imaginative with fantastic sacrifices against stronger players such as: Karpov, Anand, Topalov, and so on. In 2014 Garry Kasparov visited my country El Salvador and he crushed 6 players in a session of simultaneous games. In the link below you can watch a video of this event.

Mikhail Tal

Mikhail Tal known as the Magician from Riga was a former chess world championship. He developed new positional and tactical ideas and his sacrifices are very famous (some of them are incorrect!!). In the game knowns as "Swang Song" Tal beats Garry Kasparov in 17 moves, this game was played 1 month before his death. You can enjoy this game in the link above.
